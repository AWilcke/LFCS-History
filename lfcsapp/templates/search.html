Search Help

  • OR operator Use "or" between two keywords to search for elements containing either.

    ex: "(assistant director) or (director)" returns all people who were either director or assistant director.

  • NOT operator Use "-" before a keyword to excluse results containing it.

    ex: "smith -john" returns all people called Smith, who are not called John.

  • Parenthesis Use parentheses to separate keywords for the aforementioned operators.

  • Category operator There are 4 categories, "staff","postdoc","phd" and "associate". Use any combination of these tags to refine your search.

    ex: "smith -phd staff" returns all staff members called Smith, who did not do a PhD at the LFCS.

  • ALL operator Use "*" in any query to return all people that match the given category tags

    ex: "* phd staff" returns all people who were PhD students or staff members.
